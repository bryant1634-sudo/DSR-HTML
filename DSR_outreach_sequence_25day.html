    Do's & Don'ts

    What separates a response from a delete, and what keeps you inside the approved message
    ✓ Do This
    • Lead with Legal and Compliance; they are the champion in most won accounts
    • Name the persona's specific pain, not a generic value prop
    • Ask process questions first: how do they review and redact today?
    • Frame cost by complexity per request, not by volume
    • Keep "a reviewer approves before anything is released" in every automation claim
    • Send the breakup email; it often has the highest reply rate
    ✗ Don't Do This
    • Lead with product features in the subject line
    • Claim the platform automatically checks or blocks deletion when a legal hold exists
    • Quote pricing or savings figures; none are approved
    • Present internal targets as proven customer outcomes
    • Send a wall of text; every email needs one clear ask
    • Skip the phone and LinkedIn touches; email alone rarely sets the meeting
