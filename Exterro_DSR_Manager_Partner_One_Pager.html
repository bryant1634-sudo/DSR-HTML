The Problem

Requests arrive faster than teams can answer them

Access, deletion, correction, and opt-out demands carry strict regulatory deadlines. For most enterprises, fulfilling them is a manual scramble.

📥Personal data scattered across dozens of systems, collected by hand for every request.
⏱Hard regulatory deadlines across GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and a growing patchwork of US state laws.
🔀Documents reviewed and redacted in email and PDFs, where sensitive data leaks through.
🔍No single audit trail to show a regulator exactly how a request was handled.
